Todos Bueno Taco shop

The origins of the taco are really unknown. My theory is that it dates from the 18th century and the silver mines in Mexico, because in those mines the word “taco” referred to the little charges they would use to excavate the ore. ... And one of the first types of tacos described is called tacos de minero—miner's tacos.
