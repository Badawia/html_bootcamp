Fun At Sea Surfing

We’ve created in 2011 the first French surf school in California. We will teach all year round, in French or in English depending on your preferences! The classes take place in Half Moon Bay 30 minutes south of San Francisco where we have an official license, on a very safe spot (Princeton Jetty) close to famous Maverick’s. Trust us, you will never feel the cold water as you will enjoy some magic and exciting experiences: meeting seals & dolphins, using all the energy of your body to face the power of the ocean.

Passion for surfing

he talent with which she paints, and the passion for extreme sports are innate in Flor de Pamphilis. Both come exceptionally natural to her. Flor, also known as Pampi, is a young, talented artist from Argentina who is trail-blazing surf ­art in South America. Between painting for popular brands such as Vans and Red Bull, Pampi gave me the wonderful opportunity to gain a better understanding of her works of art, as well as the creative individual behind them.
